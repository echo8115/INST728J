ARCHITECTURE

outdoor classroom

Uses materials to define purpose.

This project aims to reinterpret outdoor classrooms. Each floor has its own purpose, and the materials chosen reinforce those purposes. The concrete first floor provides a private space for students. The second floor is public, a place where people meet and communicate. The horizontal glass wall framed with I-beams opens and lands creating a bridge to the adjacent bridge, transforming the third floor into an openair studio space.
